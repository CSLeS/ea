Events on Campus
We run six types of events and activities at LSE:
  1. Introductory talks about foundational ideas in Effective Altruism.
  2. Monthly seminars where leading academics discuss their cutting-edge global priorities research.
  3. Introductory and Advanced Fellowships and reading groups.
  4. High-impact careers workshops and coaching.
  5. Retreats with other Effective Altruism groups in various UK universities.
  6. Regular socials, pub quizzes, poker nights, inter-uni socials, and more.
